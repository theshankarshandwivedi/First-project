E-Summit is a term that typically refers to an entrepreneurial summit or conference focused on digital or online businesses. These types of events often bring together entrepreneurs, investors, industry experts, and other stakeholders in the startup ecosystem to share knowledge, network, and explore business opportunities. E-Summits can cover a wide range of topics, from marketing and branding to funding and investment, and may feature keynote speakers, panel discussions, workshops, and other interactive sessions. Some examples of E-Summits include the TiE Global Summit, the Web Summit, and the Startup Grind Global Conference.
